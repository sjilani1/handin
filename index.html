Responsive Redesign Project

Overview

The goal of this project was to re-design an existing webpage keeping in mind accessibility and usability problems

Problem

The existing issues in the interface were a lack of readibility and color contrast, lack of efficiency in reaching the intended goal (increasing customers and online orders). There was also a lack of clear organisation and text wrapping. Overall, there was also a lack of aria-labels.

Solution

My aim was to make the knead doughnut page more readable, organised and draw the users attention to important information. I re-organised the navigation bar and eliminated repeated information such as CONTACT US which had multiple buttons, replacing this with multiple options to order instead. I used color contract - yellow to highlight more important information to draw the users eye towards those areas. I reduced the writing and focused on pictures to make it more accessible and appealing to users. I added aria labels to make it more accessible and used color that is more readable.
